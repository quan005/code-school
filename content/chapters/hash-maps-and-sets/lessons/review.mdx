<ProblemStatement title="Two memory tools to remember">
  This chapter taught two main tools: sets for presence and maps for extra
  information.
</ProblemStatement>

<Intuition title="The best question to ask">
  Ask: do I only care whether something exists, or do I need a count, index, or
  match? That question usually tells you whether to use a set or a map.
</Intuition>
