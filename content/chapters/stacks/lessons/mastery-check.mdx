<ProblemStatement title="Can you rebuild the stack idea from memory?">
  This mastery check asks you to remember the main stack clues and explain why
  the top item is often the one that matters next.
</ProblemStatement>

<Intuition title="The newest unfinished thing often matters most">
  That one idea powers matching symbols, undo rules, score history, and several
  other stack patterns.
</Intuition>

<ComplexityCard
  time="Usually O(n) for common stack walks"

## Reflection

Explain in your own words when a stack is a smart choice.
