<Intuition title="The top item keeps showing up">
  In stack problems, the newest unfinished thing often matters most. That may be
  a symbol, a score, a character, an index, or a number waiting for an operator.
</Intuition>

<ComplexityCard
  time="Usually O(n) for the chapter problems"
  space="Usually O(n)"
  notes="The stack stores only the history or unfinished work the problem still needs."
/>

## Pattern Summary

- Basic stack: use the array end as the top.
- Matching symbols: push opens, pop on closes.
- Removing duplicates: compare with the top survivor.
- Score history: keep recent scores in a stack.
- Next greater: keep unanswered indexes.
- RPN: push numbers, pop two when an operator appears.
