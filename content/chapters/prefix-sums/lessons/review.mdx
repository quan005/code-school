## Pattern Summary

- Range sum: build a prefix array and subtract.
- Running totals: keep the total so far.
- Pivot index: total sum plus running left sum.
- Subarray sum equals k: prefix sums plus a hash map.
- Largest altitude: running total only.
- Product except self: left-side and right-side products.
