## Pattern Summary

- Assign Cookies: smallest helpful cookie for the smallest remaining child.
- Stock II: take every positive rise.
- Jump Game: keep the farthest reachable index.
- Lemonade Change: protect 5-dollar bills when possible.
- Partition Labels: keep the segment open until all seen letters finish.
- Gas Station: do a total check and reset the start after failure.
