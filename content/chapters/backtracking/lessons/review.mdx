## Pattern Summary

- Subsets: take or skip.
- Permutations: choose which unused item goes next.
- Phone letters: choose one letter for each digit.
- Word Search: try a path, then undo the board mark.
- Generate Parentheses: stop branches that are already invalid.
- Combination Sum: track the remaining target and stop failed branches early.
