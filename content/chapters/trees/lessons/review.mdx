## Pattern Summary

- DFS: go deep first.
- BFS: go level by level.
- Depth: take the bigger child depth.
- Same tree: compare matching nodes.
- Invert tree: swap left and right everywhere.
- Level order: BFS plus queue size.
- Search BST: use smaller-left and larger-right.
