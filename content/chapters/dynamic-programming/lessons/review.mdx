## Pattern Summary

- Fibonacci: use the two earlier answers.
- Climbing Stairs: count the ways from one step back and two steps back.
- Min Cost Climbing Stairs: choose the cheaper earlier path.
- House Robber: compare take versus skip.
- Unique Paths: use top plus left.
- Coin Change: save the minimum coins for each smaller amount.
