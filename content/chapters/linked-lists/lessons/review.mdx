<ProblemStatement title="Most linked-list problems are pointer problems">
  Traversal, search, insertion, deletion, reversal, and merging all depend on
  where one node points next.
</ProblemStatement>

<Intuition title="The chapter pattern">
  Linked lists are not about indexes. They are about following arrows and
  changing arrows safely.
</Intuition>
