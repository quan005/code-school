<ProblemStatement title="Can you rebuild the clues from memory?">
  This mastery check asks you to remember the big binary-search ideas without
  step-by-step help.
</ProblemStatement>

<Intuition title="Use order to remove work">
  The big idea is simple: if the data is in order, the middle can tell us which
  half to throw away.
</Intuition>

<ComplexityCard
  time="O(log n) for the main search pattern"
  space="O(1)"
  notes="Binary search stays fast because one comparison removes many choices."
/>

## Reflection

Explain why checking the middle is a smart move in your own words.
