## Pattern Summary

- Merge: sort and grow the current interval.
- Insert: handle before, overlapping, and after.
- Meetings: sort and detect conflicts.
- Meeting rooms: sort and track end times.
- Intersection: compare two current intervals.
- Covered intervals: sort carefully and track the farthest end.
