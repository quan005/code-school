<ProblemStatement title="Three styles to remember">
  Two pointers is not one trick. It is a family of movement patterns: inward
  traversal, unidirectional traversal, and staged traversal.
</ProblemStatement>

<Intuition title="Fast recap by problem">
  Pair Sum, Largest Container, and Palindrome use inward traversal. Shift Zeros
  uses unidirectional traversal. Next Lexicographical Sequence uses staged
  traversal.
</Intuition>
