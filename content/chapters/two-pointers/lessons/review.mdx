<ProblemStatement title="Review the pattern clues">
  A strong two-pointer problem usually gives you a structure you can trust, like
  sorted order or a mirrored string.
</ProblemStatement>

<Intuition title="Ask what each move teaches you">
  If moving left or right gives you new information, then the pattern is
  probably worth trying.
</Intuition>

<HintDrawer title="Quick self-check">
  Can you explain why moving the wrong pointer would keep the answer hidden? If
  yes, you understand the clue behind the pattern.
</HintDrawer>
