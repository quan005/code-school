<ProblemStatement title="Mastery prompt">
  You are looking at a sorted array and your current sum is too large. Which
  pointer should move, and why?
</ProblemStatement>

<Intuition title="Explain the safe move">
  The right pointer should move left, because the sorted order guarantees that
  moving it left can only make the sum smaller.
</Intuition>

<HintDrawer title="Still unsure?">
  The left pointer can only keep the same number or move to a bigger one, so it
  cannot fix a total that is already too large.
</HintDrawer>
